About this dashboard

The Residential Additions by Source dashboard displays the sources of changes in residential units in the Tahoe Region from 2013 through 2025.

Residential units may derive from new residential allocations or bonus units under the Lake Tahoe Regional Plan, or development from existing, or previously existing units that were transferred, converted, or constructed from previously assigned units.

### What's a residential bonus unit?

Under the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency (TRPA) [Code of Ordinances](https://www.trpa.gov/code-of-ordinances/), a residential bonus unit is an incentive unit provided by TRPA that may be made available to affordable, moderate, and achievable-income single and multi-family housing projects, or may be permitted to projects that remove and retire excess coverage onsite on parcels in centers (Code section 30.6.3), or that transfer existing or potential development to centers (Code section 51.5).

The Regional Plan caps the cumulative total at **2,000 residential bonus units** (1,400 from the 1987 Regional Plan and 600 from the 2012 Regional Plan that can only be used in centers). Residential bonus units are assigned based on Chapter 52 of the TRPA Code.

Current balances for residential bonus units are shown in the [Pool Balance Report](https://parcels.laketahoeinfo.org/DevelopmentRightPool/DevelopmentRightPoolBalanceReport).

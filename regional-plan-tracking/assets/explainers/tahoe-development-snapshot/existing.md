### Existing development on the ground

The residential units, commercial floor area, and tourist accommodation units that physically exist in the Tahoe Basin today.

These totals are the on-the-ground count: every unit built, every square foot of commercial space, every tourist room that has been constructed and is in active use. Counted across every parcel in the Tahoe Region.

Banked rights, reserved-but-not-yet-built allocations, and unused pool balances are tracked separately (see the sections below) — they don't show up here until construction is complete.

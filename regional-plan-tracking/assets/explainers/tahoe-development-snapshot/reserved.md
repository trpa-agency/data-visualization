### Reserved, not constructed

Allocations that have been drawn to a parcel and assigned to a permit, but the building hasn't been completed yet.

These are "in progress" — the right has been counted against the Regional Plan cap (they're no longer available in a pool), but the structure isn't built yet, so they don't appear in the on-the-ground totals above. Once construction completes, the unit shifts into "Existing development on the ground."
